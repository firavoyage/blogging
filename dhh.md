## Creator of Ruby on Rails

Hundreds of thousands of programmers around the world have built amazing applications using [Ruby on Rails](https://rubyonrails.org/). The open-source web framework that I created in 2003, and continue to develop to this day. Some of the more famous include [Github](https://github.com/), [Shopify](https://shopify.com/), [Airbnb](https://airbnb.com/), [Square](https://squareup.com/), [Coinbase](https://coinbase.com/), and [Zendesk](https://www.zendesk.com/).

## Co-owner of 37signals

[37signals](https://37signals.com/) is the company behind Basecamp, HEY, and ONCE. I own that business together with Jason Fried. We've been around since 1999.

[Basecamp](https://basecamp.com/) is the one-place-not-all-over-the-place way to organize your projects and your company. Launched in [2004](https://signalvnoise.com/archives/000542.php) as one of the first software-as-a-service applications, and used by millions of people since. It's the original Rails application.

[HEY](https://hey.com/) is a fresh approach to turning email into something you want to use, not something you're forced to deal with. A complete alternative to Gmail or Outlook for individuals and companies who want a better relationship with email. Tens of thousands of customers have embraced it since we launched in 2020.

[ONCE](https://once.com/) is a return to software products, away from services, monthly subscriptions, and shared data. Software you can own with source code you can read and change. Our first offering is [Campfire](https://once.com/campfire), super simple group chat for a one-time price. Our second offering is a *free* online book creator called [Writebook](https://once.com/writebook).

## Best-selling author

I've written four books together with Jason Fried. All on the lessons we've extracted from running 37signals in an unconventional way over the last twenty-plus years.

[REWORK](https://basecamp.com/books/rework/) is a New York Times, Wall Street Journal, and Sunday Times best seller with more than a million copies sold. It's a book about starting and running businesses a better way.

[It Doesn't Have To Be Crazy At Work](https://basecamp.com/books/calm/) is about how to run a calm company by avoiding the lure of excessive growth and making your time count for more.

[REMOTE: Office Not Required](https://basecamp.com/books/remote/) encapsulated all we'd learned working remotely for over a decade. Ahead of its time, but suddenly extremely relevant after 2020.

[Getting Real](https://basecamp.com/books/getting-real/) shared everything we learned building Basecamp and a software business back in the early days. Still on point.

## Le Mans & WEC class-winning racing driver

I've raced the [24 Hours of Le Mans](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/24_Hours_of_Le_Mans) eleven times since 2012. I won my class with Aston Martin [in 2014](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mWR0B8LvA3g), finished second in LMP2 in 2015, and third with Porsche in 2016. In 2017, I stood on [the overall podium with Rebellion Racing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5y7xCzx1FeE). I raced in the FIA [World Endurance Championship](https://www.fiawec.com/) for seven years, mostly [in LMP2 prototypes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iNQl0x6WS3M), but have also contested IMSA, ELMS, and iconic races like the Rolex 24 at Daytona and the 12 Hours of Sebring.

## Investor in Danish startups

I started investing in Danish software entrepreneurs while living in Denmark from 2020-2023. To help build the next generation of strong companies with solid roots in Denmark, I invested in [Workfeed](https://workfeed.io/), [Ziik](https://www.ziik.io/), [Turis](https://turis.app/), JumpStory, and [Servicelovers](https://servicelovers.com/). All focused exclusively on software businesses (invest in what you know!) based in Denmark and with the intent to remain there. In 2023, I was [awarded IT-Prisen](https://itb.dk/maerkesager/kapital-og-vaekst/her-er-vinderen-af-it-prisen-2023/) in part for this engagement in the Danish startup scene. (I'm not making new startup investments at the moment.)
