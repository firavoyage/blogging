# Wikipedia’s mascot

# A special friend is dropping by for Wikipedia’s 25th birthday

## Lots of surprises and no spoilers ahead

Guess who is about to join the Wikipedia party! The most playful and curious reading companion around. On 16 February, they will be popping up on certain Wikipedia pages and across a few languages when you turn on Birthday mode. So, keep your eyes peeled and get ready to celebrate 25 years of the backbone of knowledge on the internet.

[Visit the Wikipedia Portal for a sneak peek](https://www.wikipedia.org/)

![](https://wikimediafoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/WP25_PreLaunch_BALLOONS_HD_LIGHT-1.gif?w=960)

## **Wikipedia is a quarter of a century old**

Wikipedia officially turned 25 on 15 January 2026. With over 65 million articles across 300+ languages, Wikipedia informs everything from day-to-day life and AI chatbots to journalism and trivia wins.
